About us

Community Help Hub is a simple website created to connect people within the community. It allows community members to report common problems such as water shortages, road damage, health concerns, and security issues, as well as offer help and support to others. The aim of this project is to promote cooperation, awareness, and quick response to community challenges by using technology in a simple and accessible way. By bringing people together on one platform, Community Help Hub encourages everyone to take part in improving their community. This project was designed to be easy to use, mobile-friendly, and suitable for all members of the community.
